About Me

Hello, I'm Ms. Jiang, a high school math teacher with a passion for helping students understand and appreciate the beauty of mathematics. I have been teaching for 10 years and believe in creating a supportive and engaging learning environment for my students.

My Teaching Philosophy

I believe that everyone has the ability to succeed in math, and I strive to make my classes inclusive and accessible to all students. I enjoy finding creative ways to explain complex concepts and love seeing my students' "a-ha" moments.

Outside of the Classroom

When I'm not teaching, I enjoy exploring new coffee shops, reading mystery novels, and volunteering at the local animal shelter. I also love traveling and experiencing new cultures.

Get in Touch
